## Analysis & Logic

### 1. Verification via Search
In Screen 1, the AI will use the `googleSearch` tool to verify the company's website and market reputation. 
- **Value:** The right panel will display: "We've verified your market positioning against competitors..." and provide clickable citations. This builds massive authority.

### 2. Thinking-Driven Diagnostics
Screens 2 and 4 will utilize a Thinking Budget (2048-4096 tokens).
- **Reasoning:** Instead of a simple mapping, the model will reason through *why* a specific operational bottleneck (e.g., slow proposal turnaround) leads to a specific readiness gap (e.g., lack of structured CRM data).

### 3. Screen 3: The Recommendation Logic
The current hardcoded cards will be replaced by a dynamic "System Architect" agent.
- **Logic:** It will cross-reference Screen 2 priorities with a system library. If a user prioritizes "Time Savings" and identifies "Manual content work" as a blocker, the AI will synthesize a "Content Supply Chain" system recommendation.

### 4. The Streaming Experience
Using `generateContentStream`, the Right Panel will feel "alive." As the user types in the Center, the "Consultant" on the Right is "taking notes" in real-time.
